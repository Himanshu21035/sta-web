Our Story: Bridging the Education Gap

"

In 2022, we recognized a critical gap in our community—talented students and aspiring professionals in Faridabad's emerging sectors had limited access to quality, affordable technical education. Quality institutes were either too expensive or too far away.

Founded with a vision to democratize professional education, Saraswati Tally Academy emerged as the first comprehensive computer training institute serving Sector 37, Sector 91, Sehatpur, Agwanpur, and Sindhu Farm areas. We set out with a simple yet powerful mission: to provide world-class technical education at affordable prices, right in the heart of our community.

Today, we stand proud as pioneers of professional computer education in this region, having transformed over 1,200 lives and continuing to be the trusted choice for students who refuse to compromise on quality or drain their savings.
